Ciarán Hickey — Freelance web developer

Profile

I am a web developer based in Dublin. I collaborate with designers and design studios to build aesthetically driven websites with a focus on content management

With a BSc in Computer Science and an MSc in Interactive Digital Media I work in a freelance capacity building websites and implementing content management systems for a variety of clients.

I work with SMEs, start-ups, individuals and consultancies. Over the years I have provided development services to various agencies such as Red&Grey, Zero-G, Red Dog, Unthink, Open Design and Each & Other


Services

I work from supplied designs, developing them into responsive websites with full content management, using the latest modern coding techniques. Website designs can be provided in various formats such as Figma, XD, PSD, AI etc.


Technologies

I have extensive experience of front-end technologies such as HTML5, CSS/SASS, Javascript, Gulp, Git as well as open source server-side technologies such as PHP, MySQL, Wordpress & Silverstripe CMS and the bulk of my development work involves integrating and customising Content Management Systems (CMS) to allow clients complete control over their website content.
